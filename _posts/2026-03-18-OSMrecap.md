The McKinley Ocean Carbon group had a great experience at the 2026 Ocean Sciences Meeting in Glasgow, Scotland. 

We presented 3 posters and 2 talks on estimation of the magnitudes and mechanisms of the ocean carbon sink and its uncertainties. It was wonderful to connect with many collaborators and friends.
